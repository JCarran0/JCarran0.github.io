About Me

My name is Jared Carrano. I'm a full-stack developer at New Visions for Public Schools.

New Visions is a non-profit organization in New York City dedicated to ensuring all our city's students have access to a high quality education that prepares them for work or college and beyond. I'm lucky to be part of a great team working to build a robust set of data tools for administrators and teachers in our network of over 250 urban high schools.

Besides coding, I really love the great outdoors, though I find myself out there less and less. More typically I'm home enjoying my wife and our brand new baby boy, and binging on Netflix.

Plus we have two large cats.
